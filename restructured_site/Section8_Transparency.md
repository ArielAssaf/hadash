# Section 8: Transparency & Ethics - Open House (Full Text)
*Source: Site Footer & Governance*

## Accessibility Controls
The Hadash website is built with accessibility (נגישות) in mind:
- **Contrast Control**: Active.
- **Font Sizing**: Adjustable.
- **Screen Reader Support**: Implemented via WP Accessibility Helper.
- **Reporting Issues**: info@hadash.org.il

## Privacy & Data Policy
We respect our supporters' data.
- **Encryption**: Site is secured via SSL.
- **Data Usage**: Information provided via the mobilization portal is used solely for Hadash activities and mobilization.
- **Unsubscribe**: Supporters can opt-out of the "Banki" and "Maki" mailing lists at any time.

## Funding Transparency
Hadash is funded by:
- **Membership Dues**: Contributions from thousands of members.
- **Public Funding**: State funding based on Knesset representation.
- **Donations**: Small-scale donations from the public (limited by law).
- **NO Wealthy Moguls**: Hadash prides itself on being a movement of the people, not the elites.
